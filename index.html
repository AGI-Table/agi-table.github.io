USTCAGI Table Research Projects

Research

TableTime: Reformulating Time Series Classification as Training-Free Table Understanding with Large Language Models

TableTime is the first framework to reformulate multivariate time series classification as a training-free table understanding task for large language models, enabling zero-shot classification by converting time series into tabular text representations.
Research

PoTable: Towards Systematic Thinking via Stage-oriented Plan-then-Execute Reasoning on Tables

A novel table-based reasoning framework that emulates human analysts by integrating large language models with a Python interpreter to perform systematic, stage-wise analysis of tabular data.

Research

Enhancing Table Recognition with Vision LLMs: A Benchmark and Neighbor-Guided Toolchain Reasoner

This paper introduces a hierarchical benchmark and the Neighbor-Guided Toolchain Reasoner (NGTR) framework to enhance table recognition in Vision Large Language Models (VLLMs) by addressing challenges like low-quality inputs through neighbor-based tool selection and iterative refinement.
Survey

A Survey on Table Mining with Large Language Models: Challenges, Advancements and Prospects

Focusing on LLM-driven advancements, this survey offers a comprehensive overview of table mining, analyzing structural and content-related challenges, categorizing current methods, detailing key tasks and applications, and outlining future research directions in structured data understanding.
Dataset & Benchmark

ChemTable: Benchmarking Multimodal LLMs on Recognition and Understanding over Chemical Table

This paper introduces ChemTable, a comprehensive benchmark designed to evaluate multimodal large language models (MLLMs) on the complex tasks of recognizing and understanding chemical tables, aiming to advance scientific table understanding in the chemistry domain
